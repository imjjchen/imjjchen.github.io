Biography

I am a Postdoc at Kyushu University, hosted by Prof. Makoto Yokoo.

I was a research assistant at National University of Singapore, advised by Prof. Yingkai Li.

I obtained my PhD degree from the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong in Feb 2025, supervised by Prof. Minming Li. I was a visiting student at OSIPP at Osaka University from Sept 2023 to Aug 2024, advised by Prof. Takuro Yamashita. In Nov 2019, I received my M.Phil. degree from Department of Computer Science, Hong Kong Baptist University, supervised by Prof. William K. Cheung.

Interests

My research is situated at the interface between economics and computer science, especially focusing on information design (e.g., information/data pricing, signaling) and contract design.
