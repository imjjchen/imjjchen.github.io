Biography

I am now a third-year PhD student at the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong . My supervisor is Prof. Minming Li. I also closely work with Prof. Haifeng Xu and Dr. Song Zuo.

In Nov 2019, I received my M.Phil. degree from Department of Computer Science, Hong Kong Baptist University, supervised by Prof. William K. Cheung.

Interests

My research is situated at the interface between economics and computer science, especially focusing on information design (e.g., information/data pricing, signaling)
